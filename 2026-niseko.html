Trip Philosophy · 行程原则

  • Only 2 meaningful rides after arrival — Lake Toya bike check + optional Biei scenic ride.
  • After race day, prioritize recovery, nature, and family flexibility instead of training volume.
  • Minimize hotel changes and avoid overly packed schedules.
  • Use Furano as the practical base; visit Biei as day trips.
  • Keep at least one flexible half-day every day for naps, weather, or unexpected delays.

Family Travel Notes · 家庭出行须知

  • ·Do not over-plan individual days.
  • ·Always leave room for naps, weather changes, and meal delays.
  • ·Nature-focused travel works better than queue-heavy attractions with toddlers.
  • ·Driving in Hokkaido is relaxed but distances are longer than they appear on maps.
  • ·Convenience stores in Hokkaido are excellent for snacks and recovery food.
